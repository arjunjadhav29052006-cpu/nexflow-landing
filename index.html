What P2 Does

Built for
factory floors.

Not a generic inventory app. Every feature exists because a MIDC factory needed it.

📦
GRN — Goods Receipt
Log every delivery: supplier, invoice number, rate, quantity. Stock updates instantly. No duplicate entries, no paper forms going missing.
⚠️
Low-Stock Alerts on Telegram
Every morning at 8AM, owner gets a Telegram message listing all materials below reorder level. Know before production starts.
🚚
Dispatch + Digital Challan
Create dispatch orders, generate print-ready challans. If BOM is set, raw material stock deducts automatically per finished product.
📤
Tally & Zoho Export
One click generates CA-ready CSV with HSN codes, GST rates, stock groups. Works with Tally Prime and Zoho Books out of the box.
📊
Monthly Consumption Reports
Opening stock, purchases, consumption, closing stock — per material, per month. Audit-ready in seconds, not hours.
📱
QR Scanner (Pro)
Print QR labels for material bins. Scan on Android to receive or issue stock. No typing, no errors. Works on factory floor Wi-Fi or 4G.
🇮🇳
मराठी Interface
Full Marathi language toggle built-in. Your storekeeper works in Marathi — no English required. One click to switch, anytime.
🏭
Products & BOM
Define finished products with Bill of Materials. Dispatch auto-deducts raw material stock per unit. No manual recalculation.

FAQ

Common
questions.

How long does setup take?▾
2–3 days from payment to going live. We handle data import, staff setup, and a 1-hour training session on-site or via call. Most factories are fully operational within a week.
Does it work without internet?▾
It requires internet — it's a cloud application. 4G is sufficient. Anywhere your phone works, P2 works. We've tested it on factory floor networks across Pune MIDC clusters.
Is my data safe? Who can see it?▾
Your data is stored in a dedicated, encrypted database on Supabase — the same infrastructure used by major companies globally. Your data is completely isolated from other clients. We cannot access it. You can export everything to CSV at any time.
What if I need to switch to Tally or another software later?▾
Export everything to CSV in one click. You're never locked in. The Tally export already generates the exact format Tally Prime expects — with GST rates, HSN codes, and stock groups. Your CA can work with it directly.
What happens after Year 1?▾
Year 2 onwards is annual subscription only (no setup fee). AMC (Annual Maintenance Contract) is 15–20% of setup cost and covers ongoing support and updates. Founder clients keep their locked rate for 2 years.
Can my storekeeper use it? He's not very tech-savvy.▾
Yes. The GRN form is 3 fields. Dispatch is a form. The interface is designed for factory staff, not office software users. If someone can use a smartphone and fill a form, they can use P2.
